Missa Gaia / Earth Mass

with Nick Halley and Theresa Thomason

Friday, March 21 at 7:30 pm

Featuring Nick Halley, Paul Halley, the King's Chorus, Capella Regalis, and American Gospel singer, Theresa Thomason.

Written during the early years of their residency at the Cathedral of St. John the Divine in New York City, Earth Mass has been performed by the Paul Winter Consort each year since its debut. A 'mass' for Mother Nature, Missa Gaia combines the usual texts and creeds of liturgy with more unusual texts and live recordings of nature's awesome creatures. The immense sounds of humpback whales and the eerie calls of wolves are woven with contemporary jazz in the instrumental, choral, and solo vocal parts to create a mass of stunning energy and depth. The musical event of the season.
